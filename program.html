Conference Program Overview

The AALA 2026 program is designed to facilitate deep intellectual exchange and professional networking over three dynamic days. The event begins on the afternoon of September 18th with intensive pre-conference workshops, followed by a welcoming reception in the evening. The main conference days on September 19th and 20th feature a robust schedule of morning and afternoon plenary speeches delivered by world-renowned experts, interspersed with parallel sessions where researchers will present their latest findings. This structure ensures a balance between high-level theoretical insights and specific, localized research discussions.
