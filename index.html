AALA 2026

The Asian Association for Language Assessment (AALA) 2026 Conference will be held from September 18-20, 2026, at the City University of Macau. This year's theme is the 'Humanistic Approach to Assessment, Standards, Innovation, and Accountability'. We invite you to contribute to this important conversation. You can find more information on our Call for Papers page.

Why Attend?

  • Learn from leading scholars in plenary sessions and workshops
  • Exchange ideas with peers from across Asia and the world
  • Explore Macau, a UNESCO World Heritage city with rich cultural heritage and world-class facilities

Asian Association for Language Assessment

The Asian Association for Language Assessment (AALA) is a professional organization dedicated to promoting best practices in language assessment throughout Asia and beyond. Learn more about our mission and history on the official AALA website.

Macao Overview

Macao offers a unique cultural blend and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It has a compact city layout with easy transport connectivity, which is convenient for conference attendees to explore leisure and cultural attractions. Here's a glimpse into the city's key offerings:

Dining: The city has diverse dining options, ranging from authentic local eateries to high-end international restaurants with cuisines like Cantonese, Portuguese, Asian fusion, and Western.

Nightlife: The nightlife is a vibrant mix of world-class casinos, rooftop bars, nightclubs, and local pubs. You can find cozy bars with live music and stunning city views.

Tourism Infrastructure: There's strong government support for cultural tourism, historic site revitalization, and event hosting.
